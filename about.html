What is TOERSA?

TOERSA Security specializes in the handling of alcohol related issues at events and festivals. “TOERSA” is an acronym for two of the services we provide, Training and On-site Enforcement of the Responsible Service of Alcohol. Through pre-event training of all staff members and on-site enforcement, we greatly decrease the likelihood of any problems related to the sale or service of alcohol. TOERSA’s primary focus is major events and festivals that serve alcohol to the public in Ottawa and the surrounding area. These organizations typically have a special operating permit (SOP) in order to sell and serve alcohol to the public. SOPs are issued through the Liquor Control Board of Ontario (LCBO).

Why should you chosse TOERSA?

The key benefits of working with TOERSA include our in-house training and certification program and our focus on customer service at a competitive price.
Training and certification is part of the hiring criteria for TOERSA which includes: Security License, First Aid and CPR training, WHMIS, Non-Violent Crisis Intervention Training, Smart Serve Certification, Experience working where alcohol is served
In addition to ensuring that TOERSA guards receive training according to the ministry of community safety and correctinal services, we are also a registered supplier of the Ontario Security Guard Curriculum and offer security guard training for people outside the TOERSA family.
