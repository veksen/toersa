Ottawa,ON Security Guard Part-Time

The Publisher and Developer (PubDev) team is the conduit for all publisher and developer interactions with Twitch. The team represents Twitch in the market to ensure game companies are aware of and understand the various opportunities to work with Twitch. This team also evangelizes on behalf of game companies within Twitch, ensuring all departments internally are aware of what game companies would like to see from Twitch. As part of the PubDev BD team, you will manage relationships with game companies, coordinate projects across internal teams and evaluate and execute agreements on behalf of Twitch. You will also work closely with the PubDev Relations (Account Management) team. This role’s primary responsibility will be to support Twitch’s APAC initiatives and will work closely with our APAC Partnerships team. The ideal candidate will have significant experience in evaluating and framing opportunities, defining value and goals, understanding long term strategies, negotiating agreements, and managing high level relationships.The candidate should also have experience working cross-functionally across teams e.g. Product, Marketing, Legal, BizOps and Executive Teams.
